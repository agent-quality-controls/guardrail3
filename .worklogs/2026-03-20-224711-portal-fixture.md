## Summary
Added `apps/portal` to the golden fixture — a complex TS service app with two inner hexes (payments in adapters/inbound, ai-chat in adapters/outbound), mix of regular leaves and hex-in-hex in same container, and .gitkeep stubs in inner hex containers.

## Decisions
- Placed two hex-in-hex at different container levels (adapters/inbound/payments, adapters/outbound/ai-chat) to mirror RS backend's mcp/crates pattern.
- payments inner hex is fully populated with .ts files. ai-chat uses .gitkeep stubs for empty containers. Tests both patterns.
- Portal has standard Next.js dirs (app/, components/, lib/) outside hex arch — these should not be checked by arch rules.
