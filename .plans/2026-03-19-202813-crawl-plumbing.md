# Wire CrawlResult through validate orchestrators — Phase 1 plumbing

**Date:** 2026-03-19 20:28
**Task:** Thread `&CrawlResult` through all validate orchestrators without changing check logic.

## Goal
Every validate orchestrator (`rs::validate::run`, `ts::validate::run`, `hooks::validate::run`) accepts `&CrawlResult` as a parameter. The crawl happens once at the top level and the reference is passed down. No check logic changes.

## Approach

### Step-by-step plan
1. `commands/validate.rs`: Add `let crawl = crate::app::crawl::crawl(&abs_path);` before the three orchestrator calls, pass `&crawl` to each.
2. `app/rs/validate/mod.rs`: Add `crawl: &CrawlResult` param to `pub fn run()`, add import.
3. `app/ts/validate/mod.rs`: Same.
4. `app/hooks/validate.rs`: Same.
5. `main.rs`: Update all 4 call sites (run_rs_validate, handle_ts TsCommands::Validate, handle_rs RsCommands::HooksValidate, handle_ts TsCommands::HooksValidate) to crawl once and pass `&crawl`.
